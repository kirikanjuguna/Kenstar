About Us

At Kenstar, we have established a state-of-the-art infrastructure facility, equipped with a wide range of modern machines and tools driven by the latest technology. These advanced systems not only help us achieve outstanding product quality but also enable smooth and efficient loading and unloading processes.

Our highly skilled and dedicated team plays a vital role in maintaining our standards of excellence. Their expertise ensures that every product meets our commitment to superior craftsmanship.

Our product range is proudly exported across Kenya and other East African countries. Guided by a client-centric approach, we have earned the trust and loyalty of a vast number of patrons since our inception.
